Espresso

Strong black coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

Espresso mixed with steamed milk foam, often topped with cocoa powder or cinnamon.

Latte

Espresso with a lot of steamed milk and a small amount of foam.

Americano

Espresso with added hot water, resulting in a coffee similar in strength to but different in flavor from drip coffee.
